Your Trusted IT Staffing Partner in the United States

Delivering high-quality IT talent and helping businesses scale faster across the United States.

SKS Global IT Services was founded with a simple yet powerful mission: to solve the chronic shortage of qualified IT talent that holds American businesses back. Since our inception, we have grown into a leading IT staffing and consulting firm, trusted by over 150 companies – from agile startups to Fortune 500 enterprises – across healthcare, finance, retail, technology, government, and manufacturing sectors.

Unlike large, impersonal staffing agencies, we operate with a boutique approach. Every client relationship is treated as a strategic partnership. Every candidate placement is viewed as a long-term career investment. Our team brings decades of combined experience in US IT recruitment, global talent acquisition, vendor management, and technical consulting.

Headquartered in Branchburg, NJ, USA (971 US Hwy 202 N, Ste A, Branchburg, NJ 08876), we serve clients nationwide with a distributed team of recruiters, account managers, technical screeners, and compliance specialists. We are minority-owned, E-Verify participating, and fully compliant with all federal and state labor laws.
