IT Staffing Services

At Sks Global IT Services, we do not force you into a one-size-fits-all model. Instead, we offer six distinct IT staffing solutions – each designed for specific hiring scenarios. You can mix and match, switch between models, or scale up and down as your business evolves.

All our services include rigorous candidate vetting, full compliance management, transparent pricing, and dedicated account support. Read each service below to find your perfect fit.

1. Temporary IT Staffing (Contract Staffing)

Overview

Temporary IT staffing is ideal for short-term projects, seasonal workloads, employee leave coverage, or specialized initiatives that do not justify a full-time hire. We provide skilled IT professionals for durations ranging from two weeks to twelve months.

Ideal For:

  • Product launches requiring extra engineering hands
  • Cloud migration projects (3–6 months)
  • Backlog clearance for legacy systems
  • Maternity/paternity or medical leave coverage
  • Peak season support (e.g., retail, tax, healthcare enrollment)

Key Benefits:

  • Speed: First candidates in 48–72 hours
  • Flexibility: Extend or terminate with 1–2 weeks' notice
  • No Overhead: We handle payroll, taxes, benefits, workers' comp
  • Quality: Same vetting standard as direct hires
  • Compliance: Fully W2 or 1099 compliant

Why Choose Sks Global:

Many agencies send unvetted contractors. Our temporary professionals are pre-screened on technical skills and communication. We provide a 2-week “no-risk” trial – if you are unsatisfied, you do not pay.

Pricing Model: Competitive hourly rates (all-inclusive, no markups on payroll taxes).

2. Contract-to-Hire Staffing

Overview

Contract-to-hire (temp-to-perm) allows you to evaluate a candidate on your team, with your tools, on real projects – before committing to a permanent offer. Typically, contracts last 3–6 months, after which you can convert the contractor to a full-time employee with no additional placement fee.

Ideal For:

  • Companies burned by bad permanent hires
  • Roles with ambiguous requirements
  • Fast-growing startups unsure of long-term headcount
  • Candidates who prefer to “try before they buy”

Key Benefits:

  • Risk Reduction: See real performance before permanent offer
  • Cultural Validation: Assess team fit over months
  • Financial Flexibility: Contract costs are operational
  • Seamless Conversion: We handle all paperwork
  • Higher Retention: Candidates who convert stay longer

Why Choose Sks Global:

Over 85% of our contract-to-hire placements become full-time employees within 6 months. If you decide not to convert, we find a replacement at no additional cost.

Pricing Model: Hourly contract rate + reduced conversion fee (or free with longer contract).

3. Direct Hire (Permanent) Staffing

Overview

Direct hire staffing is our solution for full-time, W2 employees. We handle the entire recruitment lifecycle – from job description refinement to offer negotiation – and present only the most qualified candidates ready for permanent employment.

Ideal For:

  • Core engineering team expansion
  • Leadership roles (Tech Lead, Architect, Engineering Manager)
  • Niche skills (Mainframe, SAP, Salesforce, Embedded)
  • Companies without internal recruiting teams

Key Benefits:

  • Reduced Time-to-Hire: Average 30 days vs. 90+ in-house
  • Access to Passive Talent: Candidates not on job boards
  • 12-Month Replacement Guarantee: Free replacement if they leave within 1 year
  • Cultural & Technical Matching: We assess beyond the resume

Why Choose Sks Global:

Our senior recruiters have experience hiring for FAANG and Fortune 500 companies. We also provide salary benchmarking, offer letter templates, and relocation assistance if needed.

Pricing Model: Contingency fee (percentage of first-year salary) – only pay if you hire.

4. Payrolling and Billing Services (Employer of Record)

Overview

Already found a candidate but lack the legal entity or compliance infrastructure? Our payrolling services (employer-of-record) allow you to onboard contractors legally and quickly. We become the W2 or 1099 employer while you retain full operational control.

Ideal For:

  • Startups without US payroll setup
  • Companies hiring contractors in new states
  • Vendors needing to convert 1099 to W2 compliance
  • Staffing agencies seeking back-office support

Key Benefits:

  • Full Compliance: State tax registrations, unemployment, workers' comp, I-9, E-verify
  • Benefits Administration: Optional medical, dental, vision, 401(k)
  • Invoice & Collections: We bill your client, collect payments, pay your contractor
  • Scalable: Add contractors in any state within 24 hours
  • Audit-Ready: All records maintained for 7 years

Why Choose Sks Global:

We specialize in IT contractors, understanding hardware allowances, software licenses, IP protection, and non-compete enforcement. Our per-payroll fees are among the most competitive in the industry.

Pricing Model: Flat fee per contractor per month OR percentage of payroll.

5. Nearshore Dedicated Development Teams

Overview

Nearshore staffing involves building dedicated software development teams in Latin America (Mexico, Colombia, Brazil, Argentina, Costa Rica). These teams work exclusively for you, as an extension of your US engineering organization.

Ideal For:

  • Companies facing high US engineering costs
  • Organizations that have tried offshore (India/Philippines) with time zone challenges
  • Startups needing to scale engineering quickly without VC dilution

Key Benefits:

  • 30–50% Cost Savings
  • Time Zone Alignment: 2–5 hour overlap with EST/CST/MST
  • English Proficiency: C1-level English tested
  • Dedicated Teams: They work only for you
  • IP Protection: Full NDAs, IP assignments, secure infrastructure

Why Choose Sks Global:

We have a dedicated subsidiary and recruitment team in Latin America. We offer a 90-day guarantee – if any developer does not perform, we replace at no cost.

Pricing Model: Monthly fixed cost per developer (all-inclusive).
