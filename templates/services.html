Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers

How quickly can you fill a position?
Our average time-to-fill is 2 weeks for most positions. For urgent requirements, we can provide qualified candidates within 48 hours.
What is your replacement guarantee?
We offer a 90-day replacement guarantee for direct hire placements. If the candidate leaves within 90 days, we'll replace them at no additional cost.
Do you offer remote staffing solutions?
Yes, we specialize in remote staffing and can provide candidates from anywhere in the world.
How do you screen candidates?
We conduct technical assessments, behavioral interviews, background checks, and reference verification to ensure quality.
What is your fee structure?
Our fees are based on a percentage of the candidate's first-year salary for direct hire, or hourly rates for contract staffing.
Do you provide training for candidates?
Yes, we offer upskilling programs and training to ensure candidates meet your specific requirements.
