Privacy Policy

Your trust is the foundation of our partnership. Here's how we protect your information.

📅 Effective: January 1, 2025 🔄 Last Updated: January 1, 2025
1

Introduction

Sks Global IT Services (“we,” “us,” “our”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, submit resumes, apply for jobs, or use our staffing services.

By using our website or services, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

2

Information We Collect

Personal Information You Provide:

  • Name, email, phone, address
  • Resume, cover letter, work authorization, salary expectations
  • Company details and hiring requirements
  • Payment information

Automatically Collected:

  • IP address, browser type, OS, referring URLs
  • Pages visited, time spent, clickstream data
  • Cookies & tracking (see Section 6)
3

How We Use Your Info

For Job Seekers:

  • Match profile with job openings
  • Share resume only after consent
  • Communicate interviews & offers

For Employers:

  • Fulfill staffing requests
  • Process invoices & payments
  • Account management

All Users: Improve services, prevent fraud, legal compliance.

4

Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

Limited sharing only with:

  • Clients (with your explicit consent)
  • Service providers under confidentiality
  • Legal requests if required by law
  • Business transfers (merger, acquisition)
5

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security: TLS 1.3 encryption, firewalls, access controls, regular audits, and SOC2 Type II compliance. However, no internet transmission is 100% secure.

6

Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies for login, preferences, and analytics. You can disable them in your browser settings; some features may not work properly.

7

Your Rights

Depending on your state, you may:

  • Access & correct your data
  • Request deletion
  • Opt out of data sale (we don't sell)
  • Non-discrimination

Contact: privacy@sksglobalits.com

8

Data Retention

  • Job seekers: 3 years after last activity
  • Employers: 7 years for legal/audit
  • Analytics: 26 months (anonymized)
9

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

10

Policy Updates

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with a new "Last Updated" date. Material changes will be notified by email or banner.

11

Contact Us

Mail: Sks Global IT Services, Attn: Privacy Officer
