Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

What is your typical response time?
We typically respond within 24 hours during business days. Often much sooner! For urgent inquiries, please call us directly.
Do you sponsor H1-B visas?
We do not sponsor H1-B visas directly, but we gladly accept candidates with existing H1-B transfers or other valid work authorization like OPT, CPT, Green Card, or US Citizens.
What states do you staff in?
We provide IT staffing services across all 50 United States. Our remote and onsite capabilities allow us to serve clients anywhere in the country.
How much do your services cost?
Contract positions: competitive hourly rates. Direct hire: contingency fee ranging from 15-25% of first-year salary. We offer a free consultation to provide exact pricing.
Can I get a sample contract or MSA?
Yes! After signing a mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA), we can provide sample contracts and our Master Service Agreement for your legal team to review.
